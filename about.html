I am Mohammed Aslam Villan, a research scientist specializing in engineering of meso-micro porous assembly of nanocarbons for energy storage and photothermal conversion strategies.

Research Interests

My primary research interest lies in the engineering of meso-micro porous assembly of nanocarbons for energy storage and photothermal conversion strategies. This involves developing novel carbon-based materials with controlled porosity and surface properties to enhance energy storage capacity and photothermal conversion efficiency.
